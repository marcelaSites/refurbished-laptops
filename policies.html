Warranty Policy

At RefurbishedLaptops.ie, we stand behind the quality of our products. All our refurbished laptops come with a 90-day warranty that covers hardware failures not caused by accidental damage or misuse.

What's Covered:

  • Processor, motherboard, and chipset issues
  • RAM and storage device failures
  • Display and graphics problems
  • Keyboard, trackpad, and port malfunctions
  • Battery issues (if battery health was stated as 80% or higher at purchase)
  • Operating system and driver issues

What's Not Covered:

  • Accidental damage (drops, spills, etc.)
  • Water or liquid damage
  • Damage from misuse or abuse
  • Software issues caused by user-installed applications
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Cosmetic issues that were present at the time of purchase
  • Batteries with health stated as below 80% at purchase

How to Make a Warranty Claim:

If you experience a hardware issue covered by our warranty, please contact us via:

  • Email: thalis-yurik@hotmail.com
  • Phone: +353 83 306 1255(text message only)
  • WhatsApp: +353 83 306 1255
  • Contact form on our website

Our technical team will diagnose the issue and arrange for repair or replacement as needed. You can either bring the laptop to our Drogueda location or ship it to us (we'll cover return shipping costs for warranty repairs).

Extended Warranty Options:

We offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind:

  • 3-month extended warranty: €30
  • 6-month extended warranty: €50

Extended warranties must be purchased at the time of laptop purchase.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your refurbished laptop, we offer the following return options:

14-Day Cooling-Off Period:

In accordance with Irish and EU consumer law (Consumer Rights Act), you have the right to cancel your purchase within 14 days of receiving your laptop, without giving any reason. To exercise this right, you must inform us of your decision to cancel before the 14-day period expires.

Return Conditions:

  • The laptop must be returned in the same condition as it was received
  • All original accessories, documentation, and packaging must be included
  • No damage beyond what was present at the time of purchase
  • No personal data or accounts should remain on the device

Return Process:

  1. Contact us to inform us of your decision to return the laptop
  2. We'll provide return instructions and a return authorization
  3. Return the laptop to our Dublin location or ship it to our address
  4. Once we receive and inspect the laptop, we'll process your refund

Refund Information:

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method you used for the purchase:

  • Cash payments will be refunded in cash (in-person) or via bank transfer
  • Bank transfers will be refunded to the same account
  • Revolut payments will be refunded to the same Revolut account

Refunds will be processed within 14 days of receiving the returned laptop, subject to inspection. If the laptop is not returned in the same condition as it was received, we may deduct an amount from the refund to cover any reduction in value.

Return Shipping:

You are responsible for the cost of returning the laptop to us, unless the return is due to a defect covered by warranty. We recommend using a tracked shipping service to ensure the laptop reaches us safely.

Payment & Delivery Policy

Payment Methods:

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash: For in-person collection at our Drogueda or Clogherhead location
  • Bank Transfer (SEPA): For delivery orders or pre-payment before collection
  • Revolut: For quick and easy transfers

We do not currently accept credit/debit cards or PayPal. Payment only by transfer or cash. For delivery orders, payment must be received and cleared before the laptop is shipped.

Delivery Options:

We offer the following delivery options throughout Ireland:

  • Standard Delivery (An Post): €10 flat fee, typically 1-3 business days
  • Express Delivery (Fastway Couriers): €15 flat fee, typically next business day for most locations
  • Collection: Free collection from our Drogueda location

Delivery Process:

  1. Once payment is confirmed, we'll prepare your laptop for shipping
  2. We'll notify you when your order has been dispatched
  3. You'll receive tracking information for your shipment
  4. Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location

Collection Information:

If you choose to collect your laptop from our Drogueda location:

  • We'll notify you when your laptop is ready for collection
  • Please bring a form of ID for verification
  • If paying by cash, payment is due at the time of collection
  • If pre-paying via bank transfer or Revolut, payment must be confirmed before collection
