About Refurbished Laptops

What does "refurbished" actually mean?

Refurbished laptops are pre-owned devices that have been professionally restored to working condition. Our refurbishment process includes:

  • Complete hardware testing and repairs
  • Replacement of any faulty components
  • Thorough cleaning inside and out
  • Secure data wiping of storage devices
  • Installation of a genuine operating system
  • Final quality inspection

The result is a fully functional laptop that offers excellent value compared to buying new.

What's the difference between "refurbished" and "used"?

There's a significant difference between refurbished and simply used laptops:

Used laptops are sold as-is, without any professional testing, repairs, or data wiping. They may have hardware issues, outdated software, or contain previous user data.

Refurbished laptops undergo a comprehensive restoration process by certified technicians. They are thoroughly tested, repaired if necessary, professionally cleaned, securely wiped, and installed with a genuine operating system. They also come with a warranty for your peace of mind.

In short, refurbished laptops offer much greater reliability and value than simply used devices.

What condition are your refurbished laptops in?

We grade our refurbished laptops based on their cosmetic condition:

  • Grade A (Excellent): Minimal to no signs of use. May have very minor cosmetic marks that are barely noticeable.
  • Grade B (Good): Light signs of previous use. May have some minor scratches or marks on the case, but nothing significant.
  • Grade C (Fair): Noticeable signs of use. Will have visible scratches, scuffs, or marks on the case, but nothing that affects functionality.

Regardless of cosmetic grade, all our laptops are thoroughly tested to ensure they function perfectly. The grade is clearly indicated in each product description.

Warranty & Support

What warranty do you offer?

All our refurbished laptops come with a 30-day warranty that covers hardware failures not caused by accidental damage or misuse. If a hardware component fails during the warranty period, we will repair or replace it at no cost to you.

Our warranty includes:

  • Processor, motherboard, and chipset issues
  • RAM and storage device failures
  • Display and graphics problems
  • Keyboard, trackpad, and port malfunctions
  • Battery issues (if battery health was stated as 80% or higher at purchase)

For full details, please see our Warranty Policy.

Ordering & Payment

How do I purchase a laptop from you?

To purchase a laptop from us, follow these simple steps:

  1. Browse our catalog and select the laptop you're interested in
  2. Contact us via WhatsApp, email, phone, or our contact form to confirm availability
  3. We'll reserve the laptop for you and arrange payment and delivery/collection
  4. Make payment via your preferred method (cash for collection, or bank transfer/Revolut for delivery)

Technical Questions

What operating system is installed on your laptops?

Most of our refurbished laptops come with Windows 11 Pro pre-installed. This is a genuine, activated version of Windows with all the latest updates and security patches.

For Apple MacBooks, we install the latest compatible version of macOS.

If you prefer a different operating system (such as Windows 10 or Linux), please let us know before purchase, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

What about the battery life on refurbished laptops?

We test all laptop batteries and include the battery health percentage in our product descriptions. Most of our laptops have batteries with at least 80% of their original capacity, which typically provides 4-7 hours of use depending on the model and your usage patterns.

If a laptop has a battery with less than 80% health, this will be clearly stated in the product description. We can also replace batteries in most laptop models for an additional fee if you'd prefer a brand new battery.

Can I upgrade the RAM or storage after purchase?

Yes, we offer upgrade services for most laptop models. If you're interested in upgrading your laptop's RAM, storage, or other components, please contact us for pricing and availability.

We can also perform upgrades before you purchase the laptop. Just let us know what specifications you're looking for, and we'll provide a quote for the upgraded system.

Please note that some modern laptops (particularly ultrabooks and MacBooks) have soldered components that cannot be upgraded after manufacture.
