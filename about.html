About RefurbishedLaptops.ie

Your trusted source for quality refurbished laptops in Ireland

Our Story

RefurbishedLaptops.ie was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable laptops to students, professionals, and small businesses across Ireland while reducing electronic waste.

What started as a small operation in a Clogherhead apartment has grown into a trusted business serving customers throughout Ireland. Our founder, a computer engineer with over 9 years of experience in IT, saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between expensive new laptops and unreliable used ones.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide selection of carefully refurbished laptops from top brands like Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Apple. Our team of certified technicians ensures that every laptop we sell meets our strict quality standards and comes with a 30-day warranty for your peace of mind.

Our Refurbishment Process

01

Inspection & Assessment

Every laptop undergoes a thorough initial inspection to assess its condition and determine if it's suitable for refurbishment. We check for physical damage, screen quality, and overall functionality.

02

Hardware Testing

We run comprehensive diagnostic tests on all hardware components, including the processor, memory, storage, display, keyboard, trackpad, ports, and battery. Any failing components are replaced with quality parts.

03

Data Wiping

All storage devices undergo a secure data wiping process that meets industry standards, ensuring that no previous user data remains on the device. This process is certified and documented.

04

Cleaning & Cosmetic Restoration

Each laptop is thoroughly cleaned inside and out. We remove dust from cooling systems, clean keyboards and screens, and address minor cosmetic issues to ensure the laptop looks as good as possible.

05

OS Installation & Updates

We install a genuine operating system (typically Windows 11) and ensure all drivers and software are up to date. We also install essential software and security updates to protect your device.

06

Final Quality Check

Before any laptop is offered for sale, it undergoes a final 20-point inspection to ensure everything is working perfectly. Only laptops that pass this rigorous testing receive our quality certification.
